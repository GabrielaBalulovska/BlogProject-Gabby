About Me

My name is Gabriela Balulovska, I’m a 19 year old second year university student in Wellington. I’m originally born in Macedonia and moved to New Zealand when I was two, I speak fluent Macedonian which makes English my second language. I lived in Auckland my whole life but then moved to Wellington in 2019 to study Design at Victoria University.



My Major last year was Communications Design and now is Social Innovation. As I have done mostly graphic design throughout my school years the Web Design paper seemed like something new and different. I really enjoyed designing websites in other classes like the Graphic design paper, which didn’t include coding, therefore I thought it would be something interesting to gain knowledge in and explore.
